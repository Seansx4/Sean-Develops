Brochure Websites

Brochure websites are exactly that, a brochure. These lightweight websites do not rely on complex data management systems and are ideal for providing local businesses with a web presence. Consumers are returning to favouring websites over apps and social media profiles, meaning a web presence is more important than ever. According to a recent survey, 84% of today's consumers think a business with a website is more credible than a business with only a social media profile.

Website Building Tools

The majority of todays websites are built using website building tools, such as WordPress. These tools are fantastic for large scale or complicated website projects, but due to their cost, I believe they are completely unneccsary for brochure websites. The cheapest monthly WordPress subscription is €23, meaning owners of a WordPress website pay over €250 a year to simply own a website.

Proffesional web developers expense this cost to clients, passing it off as the price you have to pay to own a website, when in fact it is the price you have to pay to own a website built using their tools of choice. Furthermore, this price is prior to paying for a custom domain registration or SSL security certificate (I explain what this means here). This is why I custom code each website I create from scratch. By forgoing the use of a website building tool, the websites I create avoid the hefty montly subscription price associated with website building tools.

Websites

As mentioned above, the websites I create are built using my own custom code. As such, many of these websites share a similar structure. This is to ensure they are user friendly on mobile, tablet and desktop devices. While the websites may be similar in terms of layout, it is the design that sets them apart and creates a unique site.

My Approach

A common practice in web development is to carry out a design first approach with clients. This approach often leads to extended discussions over minor details, and typically favours developers who are paid by the hour. My approach is unique in the manner I fully create a website before reaching out to a business. This approach functions to reduce the design phase, saving both money and time. The main objective with a brochure website is to establish a web prescence, and once this is achieved, design can then follow on a smaller scale.
