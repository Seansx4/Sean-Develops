Brochure Websites

Brochure websites are exactly that, a brochure. They are lightweight websites which do not rely on complex architectures and data management systems in the way websites like Facebook and Amazon would. Typically, brochure websites are well suited to local businesses who require a web presence. A simple web presence is extremely important in todays mobile culture, and even more so with the recent trend of consumers returning to favour a website over an app or social media profile. In fact, 84% of todayâ€™s consumers think a business with a website is more credible than a business with only a social media profile (source:10 reasons why you need a website for your small business).

Website Building Tools

The vast majority of todays websites are built using website building tools, such WordPress or Square Space. These powerful tools allow web developers to quickly design and create websites for their clients. While these tools have undoubtedly made web development more accesible, and helped to raise the standard of todays websites, they are subscription based, and so come at a cost. The cheapest WordPress plan for example is 23 euro a month. This means users pay over 250 euro a year to simply own an often quiet limited website. Proffesional web developers expense this cost to the client, passing it off as the price to own a website nowadays, when instead it is the price to pay to own a website built using their tools of choice. Furthermore, this price is prior to paying for a custom domain or SSL security. While I truly believe website building tools are fantastic, in my opionon they are unnnescary for brochure websites. This is why I custom code each website I create from scratch. As I own the code, the websites I create forgo the hefty hosting price associated with website building tools.
