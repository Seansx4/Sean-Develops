The Rootless Project is a collabroation run by Jake and Sean.

We are two outdoor enthusiasts currently based in Dublin, Ireland. TRP was set up as an artistic venture for our favourite clips, ranging from everyday scenes to travel destinations.

Over time we started to work with small to medium businesses filming and editing promtional videos.
